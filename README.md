# Qolda Pocket powered by ISSAI's Qolda AI
Qolda AI is a powerfull LLM, that specifies on Kazakh language support. Our Product **Qolda Pocket** is a telegram bot which powered on this LLM. It is more accesable for people to use AI on their phone, rather than set it up on powerfull PC. That is why we came up with such idea, to make the best Kazakh Langugae Model more accesible.
# Why we did it?
We acted under the **Local Impact Hackaton**, our topic was **"AI in Daily Life"**.
